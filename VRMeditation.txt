# 🎤 IMPORTANT VIVA QUESTIONS

---

## ❓ What is terrain?

👉 Terrain is a Unity component used to create natural landscapes like hills and mountains.

---

## ❓ Why use skybox?

👉 Skybox creates environmental background and improves immersion.

---

## ❓ Why use directional light?

👉 Directional light simulates sunlight.

---

## ❓ What is Audio Source?

👉 Audio Source is a Unity component used to play sound clips.

---

## ❓ Why use ambient sounds?

👉 Ambient sounds improve realism and relaxation.

---

## ❓ What creates the waterfall?

👉 A scaled cube with blue water material simulates the waterfall.

---

## ❓ Why smooth terrain?

👉 To create realistic natural hills and remove sharp spikes.

---

## ❓ What is the purpose of VR meditation environment?

👉 To create a peaceful immersive virtual environment for relaxation.

---

## ❓ Why use Built-In Render Pipeline?

👉 It is lightweight, stable, and suitable for educational projects.
